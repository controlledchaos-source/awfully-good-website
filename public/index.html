Our (Awkward) Origin Story

We're Not Doctors. We're Just Misunderstood.

Awfully Good started because we were tired of pretending to be okay. We're not. None of us are. And that's kind of the point.

Our founder was that weird kid who didn't fit in, took too long to decide things, and overthought conversations from 2007. So they decided: instead of fixing themselves, what if they could just... think better?

Lion's Mane isn't a miracle cure. It's a tool for weirdos. For people whose brains work different. For people who deserve to feel sharp without feeling fake.

We built Awfully Good for the misfits, the overthinkers, and everyone who's ever felt like they were one bad day away from losing it. (You're welcome.)

Questions We Get Asked (A Lot)

Will this make me normal? +
No. It'll make you sharper, more focused, and better able to advocate for your weirdness. Normal is overrated anyway.
How long until I feel something? +
Most people notice improvements in focus and clarity within 2-3 weeks. Some feel it sooner. Give it time. Your brain didn't break overnight.
Is this a scam? +
Nope. We're selling a real product with real benefits. We're not claiming to cure your existential dread, but we can help you think through it more clearly.
Can I take this with other stuff? +
Probably, but talk to your doctor first. We're a supplement company, not your therapist. (Though honestly, we should charge like one.)
What if I hate it? +
30-day money-back guarantee. No questions. We get it. Not everything works for everyone.
