How much Lion's Mane per serving?
1000mg pure Lion's Mane extract. That's 2–3x stronger than most alcohol-based tinctures, which lose potency during extraction. This is the real dose, not a marketing dose.
When will I feel it?
Most people notice something in weeks 1–2. Full clarity hits around day 30, when neuroplasticity has had time to compound. Consistency is everything. Don't skip days and expect week 4 results at week 1.
Why alcohol-free?
Alcohol extraction is cheaper but damages the beta-glucans and hericenones you actually want — the compounds responsible for the effects. Water extraction is more expensive and more effective. We chose the latter. You're welcome.
Any side effects?
None. Lion's Mane has a strong safety profile across 50+ peer-reviewed studies. The only thing you might experience is your brain working the way it was supposed to. Some people find that mildly alarming at first.
